When Adversaries & Look Beyond Earth

by Brandon Bailey
SeriesEzra Systems Seminar · Fall '25
VenueDuffield 146 · 4:30 PM EST
Length58 min · Q&A included
Abstract — read in 3 min

As commercial activity in low-earth orbit accelerates, the perimeter of what we must defend has begun to stretch — past the atmosphere, into contested space. This talk surveys the emerging adversarial landscape above our heads: jamming, signal spoofing, and the cyber-physical vulnerabilities of satellites that were never built with hostile neighbors in mind.

Drawing on recent engagements at The Aerospace Corporation, Bailey walks through a year of red-team exercises against on-orbit systems and outlines the system-engineering patterns that appear to hold up under pressure — and the ones that don't.
